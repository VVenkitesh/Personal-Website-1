(Nonfiction) Book Recommendations

If you're a fan of nonfiction, here are a few books I think are good!

    "I Am Malala: How One Girl Stood Up For Education and Changed the World" by Malala Yousafzai [Note that there is both a Young Readers edition and the original version of this book.]
    "Chasing Lincoln's Killer" by James L. Swanson
    "Night" by Elie Wiesel
    "Anne Frank: Diary of a Young Girl" by Anne Frank
