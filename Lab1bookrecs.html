(Fiction) Book Recommendations

I like reading in my free time- so here are a few books that I think are really interesting!

List of Books:

    "This is Where it Ends" by Marieke Nijkamp [my current favorite!]
    "The Book Thief" by Markus Zusak
    "The Boy in the Striped Pajamas" by John Boyne
    "Animal Farm" by George Orwell
    "The Wednesday Wars" by Gary D. Schmidt [This may only be a middle-grade novel, but don't underestimate it- it's a good book!]
